Water Resistance: The water-resistant properties of PVC make it perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and other moisture-prone areas. Affordability: PVC flooring provides a cost-effective alternative to traditional flooring materials like wood or stone without compromising on quality or aesthetics. Variety of Designs: Available in a wide range of patterns, colors, and textures, PVC flooring can mimic the look of wood, marble, and other premium materials.
